====== 21. Impangako na yan Apo Dioh ye Māngiligtah ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-01.jpg}}


The first promise of the coming Messiah came to Adam and Eve. God promised that a descendant of Eve would be born who would crush the snake’s head. The snake who deceived Eve is Satan. The promise means the coming Messiah would defeat Satan completely.

Noon pa mang una, plano na ng Diyos na magpadala ng isang Tagapagligtas sa mga tao. Noong nagkasala sina Adan at Eba, ipinangako na ito ng Diyos. Sinabi niya sa ahas, “Ang anak ng babae ang dudurog sa ulo mo. Ikaw naman ang kakagat sa sakong niya.” Ang ahas na nanlinlang kay Eba ay si Satanas. Ang ibig sabihin ng Diyos, mayroong ipapanganak ang siyang tatalo kay Satanas.

Ha hatew po, hiyay Apo Dioh, inihip nayna a mangitobol yan Māngiligtah nin tatao. Ha nagkahalanan yay Adan boy Eba, yati ye impangako nan Apo Dioh kanla, hinabi na a, “Maabot lano ye panaon, ket magha kanlan lalahi yo ye mamitpit nin ō nan bikat.” Hiyay labay habiyen nin bikat ket hiyay Satanas a nanalingo kanan Eba ha hatew.

BT - In time-past still, Apo God, he thought/decided that he will-send Savior of people. When Adan and Eba sinned, this is what Apo God promised to-them, he-said, “Will-arrive later the time, one of your(PL) clan is the one who will-crush the head of the snake.” The meaning to say of snake is Satanas who deceived Eba in time-past.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-02.jpg}}


God promised Abraham that through him all people groups of the world would be blessed. This blessing was the coming Messiah who would one day provide the way of salvation for all people groups.

Ipinangako din ng Diyos kay Abraham na dahil sa kanya ay makakatanggap ng pagpapala ang mga lahat ng tao. Ang pagpapalang ito ay ang pagdating ng Tagapagligtas.

Hiyay Apo Dioh, impangako na met kanan Abraham, a wana, “Makaoli kamo, inged ko hilay kaganaan a lalahi.” Yatin kangedan ket hiyay panlomateng nin Māngiligtah.

BT - Apo God, he promised also to Abraham, he-said, “By-means-of you, I will-do-good-to all clans.” This goodness is the arriving of the Savior.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-03.jpg}}


God promised Moses that He would raise up another prophet like Moses. This was another reference to the coming Messiah.

Ipinangako naman ng Diyos kay Moises na mayroong darating na isang propetang katulad niya. Ito ay isa ring pangako ng Diyos tungkol sa Tagapagligtas.

Hiyay Apo Dioh, impangako na met kanan Moises a mangitobol yan maghan propita a omon kana. Magha po yati kanlan impangako nan Apo Dioh a tongkol kanan Māngiligtah.

BT - Apo God, he promised also to Moises that he will-send a prophet like him. This is one also of what Apo God promised about the Savior.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-04.jpg}}


God promised David that the Messiah would be one of David’s own descendants.

Ipinangako ng Diyos kay David na isa sa mga magiging kaapu-apuhan niya ang Tagapagligtas.

Ha hatew, impangako na met Apo Dioh kanan Poon David, a magha kanlan aapo na ye Māngiligtah, a wana, “Hiyay impangako ko kanan Adan boy Eba, Abraham boy Moises, ket hiya met ateed kammo, ta mangibat ya lano ha aapo mo ye Māngiligtah.”

BT - In time-past, Apo God promised also to Ruler David, that one of his descendents is the Savior, he-said, “What I promised to Adan and Eba, Abraham and Moises, it also to-you, for from later your descendents is the Savior.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-05.jpg}}


Through the prophet Jeremiah, God promised that He would make a New Covenant, but not like God’s Covenant with Israel. In the New Covenant, God would write His law on the people’s hearts, the people would know God, they would be His people, and God would forgive their sins. The Messiah would start the New Covenant.

Sa pamamagitan ni Propeta Jeremias, ipinangako ng Diyos na gagawa siya ng isang Bagong Kasunduan. Iba ito sa kasunduan niya sa mga taga-Israel. Sa Bagong Kasunduan, isusulat ng Diyos sa puso ng mga tao ang mga gusto niyang gawin nila. Ang mga taong ito na magpapasyang kilalanin ang Diyos ang siyang magiging bayan ng Diyos. Patatawarin ng Diyos ang mga kasalanan nila. Sa darating na Tagapagligtas magsisimula ang Bagong Kasunduan.

Ha hatew, impahabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Jeremias, a wana, “Manyag akon bayon kahondoan kanlan tatao. Ha hatew, hiyay bibilin ko ket inholat ko ya ha dadapah. Noba haanin, iholat koynay bibilin ko ha poho la. Ket hilay mamigbig kangko ye diyagen kon pagtao ko. Ket patawaden ko hila ha kakahalanan la.” Mandogi yati ha panlomateng nin Māngiligtah.

In time-past, Apo God caused-to-speak through Jeremias, he-said, “I will-make a new covenant with people. In time-past, my commands I wrote on stones. But now, I will-write my commands in their hearts. And those recognizing/acknowledging me are the ones I will-make my people. And I will-forgive them of their sins.” This will-begin at the arrival of the Savior.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-06.jpg}}


The coming Messiah would be a prophet, a priest, and a king. A prophet is a person who hears the words of God and then proclaims God’s words to the people. The coming Messiah would be the perfect prophet.

Sinabi din ng mga propeta na ang Tagapagligtas ay magiging propeta, pari at hari. Ang propeta ay ang mga taong nakikinig sa sinasabi ng Diyos at sinasabi naman nila ito sa mga tao. Ganito din ang gagawin ng darating na Tagapagligtas.

Hinabi la po nin popropita a hiyay Māngiligtah, ket magha yan propita, padi, boy poon. Hiyay propita, magha yan taon mangiabot kanlan tatao nin nange na kanan Apo Dioh. Wanabay met lano ye diyagen nan lomateng a Māngiligtah. Ta hiyay Māngiligtah ye genap a propita.

BT - The prophets still said that the Savior, he is a prophet, priest, and ruler. The prophet, he is a person making-reach to people what he heard to Apo God. Like-that also later is what will-be-done when the Savior arrives. For the Savior is the perfect/complete prophet.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-07.jpg}}


A priest is someone who makes sacrifices to God on behalf of the people to take away their sins. Priests also pray to God for the people. The coming Messiah would be the perfect high priest who would offer himself as a perfect sacrifice to God.

Ang paring-Judio naman ang naghahandog para patawarin ng Diyos ang mga tao. Ipinapanalangin din nila ang mga tao. Kaya, ihahandog din ng Tagapagligtas ang sarili niya para patawarin ng Diyos ang lahat ng tao.

Hiyay Māngiligtah ye maghay padi. Hiyay padi nin Jujudio ye ampangihagpa kanan Apo Dioh ta omen mapatawad ye kakahalanan nin tatao boy hiya met ateed ye mangipakigwang kanla. Hiyay Māngiligtah, hadili nan laman ye ihagpa na ta omen mapatawad ye kakahalanan nin tatao. Ta hiyay Māngiligtah ye genap a padi.

BT - The Savior is a priest. A priest of the Jews is the one offering (lit. setting-down) to Apo God in order that the sins of the people will-be-forgiven and he also is the one praying-for them. The Savior, own body is what he will-offer (lit. set-down) in order that the sins of people will-be-forgiven. For the Savior is the perfect/complete priest.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-08.jpg}}


A king is someone who rules over a kingdom and judges the people. The coming Messiah would be the perfect king who would sit on the throne of his ancestor David, who would reign over the whole world forever, and who would judge honestly and make the right decision every time.

Ang mga hari naman ang namumuno sa mga kaharian at sila din ang humahatol sa mga tao kung tama o mali ang ginawa nila. Ganito din ang gagawin ng darating na Tagapagligtas. Siya ang magpapatuloy sa paghahari ni Haring David. Maghahari siya sa buong mundo. Hahatulan din niya ang mga ginagawa ng mga tao. Siya ay makatarungan at tama ang ginagawa niyang mga desisyon.

Hiyay Māngiligtah, magha yan poon. Hiyay poon, hiya met ye ampanohga boy ampanakop kanlan tatao ha panakopan na. Hiyay Māngiligtah ye manohga kanlan tatao ihti ha babe lota, ket matoynong ye panohga na. Boy mikno ya lano ha pamiknoan nan Poon David a kaapoapowan na boy manakop yan homain anggaan ha babe lota. Ta hiyay Māngiligtah ye genap a poon.

BT - The Savior, he is a ruler. A ruler, he also is judging and having-jurisdiction-over people in his having-jurisdiction-area. The Savior, is the one who will-judge the people on earth, and righteous is his judging. And he will-sit on the sitting-on-place of Ruler David who is his ancestor and he will-have-jurisdiction with no ending on earth. For the Savior is the perfect/complete ruler.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-09.jpg}}


The prophets predicted the birth of the coming Messiah. Malachi foretold that a great prophet would come before the Messiah came. Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin. Micah said that he would be born in the town of Bethlehem.

Sinabi ng mga propeta ang maraming bagay na mangyayari sa buhay ng darating na Tagapagligtas. Sinabi ni Malakias na may isang makapangyarihang propeta na mauuna sa Tagapagligtas. Sinabi naman ni Isaias na ipapanganak ng isang birhen ang Tagapagligtas. Si Mikas naman ang nagsabi na sa Betlehem ipapanganak ang Tagapagligtas.

Hilay popropita, malabong a babagay ye inhola la a mangalyadi ha bi-ay nan Māngiligtah. Hinabi nan Malakias, a wana, “Bayo lomateng ye Māngiligtah, mona ya po a lomateng ye makapalyadiyan a propita.” 
Hiyay Isaias, hinabi na met, a wana, “Ianak ya lanon maghay balatang ye Māngiligtah." 
Hiyay Mikas, inhola na met, a wana, “Maianak ya lanoy Māngiligtah ha banwan Betlehem.”

BT - The prophets, many things are what they predicted which will-happen in the life of the Savior. Malakias spoke, he-said, “Before the Savior will-arrive, first still will-arrive the powerful prophet.”
Isaias, he spoke also, he-said, “The Savior will-be-given-birth later by a young-unmarried-woman.” 
Mikas, he predicted also, he-said, “The Savior will-be-born later in the city of Betlehem.”


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-10.jpg}}


The prophets also prophesied about the Messiah’s ministry. Isaiah said he would live in Galilee, comfort the broken-hearted, proclaim freedom to captives, and release to prisoners. He also predicted that the Messiah would heal sick people, including those who could not hear, see, speak, or walk.

Sinabi din ni Isaias na sa Galilea titira ang Tagapagligtas. Aaliwin nila ang mga nasasaktan. Palalayain niya ang mga binihag at pakakawalan niya ang mga bilanggo. Sinabi din niyang pagagalingin ng Tagapagligtas ang mga maysakit, pati ang mga hindi makarinig, hindi makakita, hindi makapagsalita o hindi makalakad.

Inhola la po nin popropita no way-omen ye pagbi-ay nan Māngiligtah. Hiyay Isaias, inhola na a, “Komonin ya lanoy Māngiligtah ha plobinhyan Galilea. Ket liwaliwaen na hila angkalele. Boy ipatanda na a malihway hilaynay naipoh boy hilay nakapidiho.” 
Hinabi na po nin propita Isaias, “Paitaahen na hilay ampaghakit, omel, teek, kapkap boy lompo.”

BT - The prophets still predicted as to how is the living of the Savior. Isaias, he predicted, “The Savior will-reside later in the province of Galilea. And he will-comfort those being-sad, he will-cause-to-know that free are those who became-slave and those imprisoned.”
Isaias still said, “He will-heal those being-sick, can not speak, deaf, blind, and crippled.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-11.jpg}}


Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be hated without reason and rejected. The Psalms foretold the Messiah’s that people would gamble for his clothes and he would be betrayed by a friend. (Psalms are Jewish worship songs in the Scriptures.) Zechariah foretold that the friend would be paid thirty pieces of silver for betraying him.

Sinabi din ni Isaias na magagalit ang ibang mga tao sa Tagapagligtas. Hindi sila maniniwala dito. Sinabi pa ng ibang mga propeta na magsusugal ang mga taong papatay sa Tagapagligtas. Sinabi pa nila na isa sa mga kaibigan niya ang magtataksil sa kanya. Si Zacarias ang nagsabi na ang bayad sa pagtataksil na ito ay 30 salaping pilak.

Noba hinabi na met propita Isaias a kapootan la ya lano boy ahe la ya balayen a hiyabay ye Māngiligtah. Hinabi la po a, “Lipotan yan maghay gayyem na.” 
Hiyay Zakarias, inhola na met, a wana, “Main lanon mangipaloke kana ha alaga nin 30 a pilak.”
Hilay kanayon a popropita, hinabi la met a, “Pateyen la yan tatao boy payhogalan lay babado na.”

BT - But the prophet Isaias still said that they will-be-angry-with him later and they will- not -recognize him that he is the Savior. They still spoke that, “He will-be-betrayed by a friend of his.”
Zakarias, he predicted also, he-said, “There is [someone] later who will-do-evil/betray to-him for price of 30 money/silver.”
Other prophets, they also said, “People will-kill him and they will-gamble-for his clothes.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-12.jpg}}


The prophets also told about how the Messiah would die. Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be spit on, mocked, and beaten. Then he would be pierced and hung on a tree where he would die.

Sinabi na din ng mga propeta kung paano mamamatay ang Tagapagligtas. Sinabi na Isaias na duduraan siya, pagtatawanan at bubugbugin. Pagkatapos, siya ay sasaksakin ng sibat habang nakapako sa krus hanggang mamatay siya.

Impatanda la po nin popropita no way-omen ye pagkamatey nan Māngiligtah. Inhola nan Isaias a “Lod-an la yay Māngiligtah, omihen boy bogbogen laya. Pangayadi, binghoen la ya legan nakapako ya ha kodoh anggan ha matey ya. Ket matey ya ha pietan nin lowan māgkahalanan.”

BT - The prophets still caused-to-know as to how is the dying of the Savior. Isaias predicted that “They will-spit-on the Savior, they will-mock and beat him. Afterwords, they will-stab him while he is-nailed to the cross until he will-die. And he will-die between two sinners.”


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-13.jpg}}


The Messiah would be perfect, having no sin. He would die to receive the punishment for other people’s sin. His punishment would bring peace between God and people. For this reason, it pleased God to crush the Messiah.

Walang kasalanang nagawa ang Tagapagligtas. Ang parusang tatanggapin niya ay ang parusa para sa ibang tao. Pero dahil dito maaari nang magkasundo ulit ang Diyos at ang mga tao. Kaya tinanggap ng Diyos na maparusahan ang Tagapagligtas.

Hiyay Māngiligtah, homain yan kahalanan. Noba mapadohaan yan teed oli ta hiyabay ye mananggap nin padoha ha kakahalanan nin tatao. Olin yati, main ana katanaan ye tatao kanan Apo Dioh.

BT - The Savior, he has no sin. But he will-be-punished anyway because he is the one who will-receive the punishment of the sins of people. Because of this, people now have peace to Apo God.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-14.jpg}}


Not only would the Messiah die, but the prophets foretold that God would raise him from the dead. Through the Messiah’s death and resurrection, God would complete His plan of salvation and start the New Covenant.

Hindi lang basta mamamatay ang Tagapagligtas. Pero ipinangako ng Diyos na mabubuhay siya ulit. At dahil sa kamatayan at muling pagkabuhay ng Tagapagligtas, matutupad na ang plano ng Diyos na iligtas ang mga tao mula sa kasalanan. Pwede na ulit silang gumawa ng isang Bagong Kasunduan.

Hiyay kakahalanan lan tatao ye hangkan nin kamateyan nin Māngiligtah. Noba aliwan bengat angga ihtew, ta inhola la met nin popropita a bi-ayen na yan oman Apo Dioh. Ket oli ha pagkamatey boy pagkabi-ay nan oman nin Māngiligtah, nignap anay inihip nan Apo Dioh a kaligtahan nin tao. Ket nandogi yaynay bayon kahondoan nan Apo Dioh kanlan tatao.

BT - The sins of people are the reason of the death of the Savior. But not just until there, for the prophets also predicted that Apo God will-make- him -alive again. And because of the death and coming-to-life again of the Savior, became-complete/perfect what Apo God thought/decided which is the salvation of a person. And began already the new agreement of Apo God to people.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-15.jpg}}


God showed the prophets many things about the coming Messiah, but the Messiah had not come yet. More than 400 years after the last of these prophecies was given, when the time was perfect, God sent the Messiah into the world.

Ipinakita ng Diyos sa mg propeta ang maraming bagay tungkol sa darating na Tagapagligtas. Pero hindi pa dumarating noon ang Tagapagligtas. Mahigit 400 taon pa ang lumipas bago ipinadala ng Diyos ang Tagapagligtas ng lahat ng tao.


Malabong ye impakit nan Apo Dioh kanlan popropita na tongkol ha panlomateng nan Māngiligtah. Noba maigit anan 400 a taon po ye malabah bayo ya lomateng ye Māngiligtah kanlan tatao ihti ha babe lota. Ta ha panaon a intipan nan Apo Dioh, itobol naynay Apo Jesus a Māngiligtah.

BT - Many are what Apo God showed to his prophets about the arrival of the Savior. But more than 400 years still will-pass before the Savior will-arrive to the people here on earth. For at the time set by Apo God, he will-send Apo Jesus who is the Savior.

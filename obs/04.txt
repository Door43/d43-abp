====== 4. Hiyay Pangako nan Apo Dioh kanan Abraham ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?direct&}}

Ha kaboyotan nin panaon pangalabah nin lanab, linomabong ana man ye tatao ihti ha babe-lota boy mamaghan kalahi po ye habi la. Noba ahe hila nanombong ha bilin nan Apo Dioh a mapaytayak hilan makew ha kaganaan a lologal ha babe-lota. (Gen. 11:1).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?direct&}}

Malambong hila. Kaya-bay napaymamaghaan lay mangipaideng nin matagay a nagmamatondol a todi, ta labay lay  miabot ha langit. Nakit nan Apo Dioh yatin kapahalombangan boy tanda na a maloloke hila po lano. (Gen. 11:2-6).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?direct&}}

Ket hiyay dinyag  nan Apo Dioh, pinaylolombo nay hahabi la ta-omen hilayna ahe mapaytalohtaloh. Ket oli ha ahe la paykataloh, ket napaytayak-tayak hilaynan nakew ha kanayon a lologal. (Gen. 11:7-9).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?direct&}}

Pangalabah nin ginatoh a taon, main maghay laki a nagngalan Abram. Ket hiyay ngalan nin ahawa na, Sarai. Hiyay Abram, kinatongtong na yan Apo Dioh, a wana, “Lakwanan moy logal mo boy hilay papaltido mo, ket makew kawon miahawa ha logal a ipakit ko kammo.’’ (Gen. 12:1).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?direct&}}

Ket hiyay Abram boy ahawa na, nanige hilan palakew ha logal a ipakit nan Apo Dioh kanla. Ha niabot hila ihtew, nagpangako yay Apo Dioh kana, a wana, (Gen.12:4-6).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?direct&}}

“Hilatin kaganaan a lologal a angkakit mo, ibyay ko kammo a tawiden lan lalahi mo."

“Hilatin kaganaan a lologal a angkakit mo, ibyay ko kammo a tawiden lan lalahi mo.” (Gen. 12:7).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?direct&}}

Ket hilay lalahi mo, palabongen ko hila boy mag-ilyadi hilan mayadet a nahyon. Ket hikoyna haanin ye Apo Dioh yo. Boy inged kata ta omon la matandaan nin kalabongan a tatao ye ngalan mo. Boy inged ko hila met ye manginged kamo, noba hilay manoboy kammo, toboyan ko hila met. Ket makaoli ha lalahi mo a inged ko, inged ko hila met ye kaganaan a nanahyon.’’(Gen 13:16).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?direct&}}

Ket inihip nan Abram, a wana, “Ampamteg ako a topaden nan Apo Dioh ye kaganaan a impangako na kangko.” Kaya-bay hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Abram, “Oli ha pamteg mo kangko, an-ibilang katan matoynong”, wana.  Noba hiyay maidap, homain hila po a anak. Ket no wanabay, way-omon awod a matopad ye pangako nan Apo Dioh a hiyay Abram ye pangibatan nin mayadet a nahyon?

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?direct&}}

4.9 Ha maghay mangaamot, hiyay Abram, nahagana na yay Melkizedek a magha yan padi nin Katagayan a Dioh. Ket hiyay Melkizedek, in-inged na yay Abram. Hinabi na kana a, “Hiyay Apo Dioh a Katagayan a nagkonin nin langit boy babe-lota, inged na ka Abraham.” Ket hiyay Abraham, in-ibyay na kanan Melkizedek ye ikamapo nin kaganaan a pagkonin na. (Gen. 14:18-20)

One day, Abram met Melchizedek, the priest of God Most High. Melchizedek blessed Abram and said, “May God Most High who owns heaven and earth bless Abram.” Then Abram gave Melchizedek a tenth of all he owned.

//A Bible story from: Genesis 11-15//

====== 3. Hiyay Lanab ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg}}

Many years later, many people were living in the world. They had become very wicked and violent. It became so bad that God decided to destroy the whole world with a huge flood.

Ha kaboyotan nin panaon, legan anlomabong hilay tatao ihti ha babe lota, ket lalalo hila anlomoke. Kaya-bay nahabin Apo Dioh, a wana, “Damaen koy matiboen babe lota makaoli ha makhaw a tobat a lanab.”  

Pamakayarin makarang nga panawun, kaban mallumakêy mani tawu sa babun luta, suspu silan dinumawak. Kabay nasabin Bapan Namalyari, “Sirên kinay buun babun luta sa kapamilatan bapan dagul lanab.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg}}

But Noah found grace in God’s eyes. He was the only righteous man among all the wicked people. God told Noah about the flood He was planning to send. He told Noah to build a huge boat called an ark.

Noba main maghay laki a manombong kanan Apo Dioh. Kaya-bay angkaaliketan na yan Apo Dioh. Hiyay ngalan na, ket Noe.  Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Noe, “Noe, lanaben koy matiboen babe lota. Kaya-bay manyag kan mayadet a bangka.”

Nuwa dilag gisay liyaki ya kapapaidu kan Bapan Namalyari. Si lagyu na, Noe. Gisa yan mapanunul kan Bapan Namalyari. Sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe, “Noe ya, lanabên kuy buun babun luta. Kabay manyag kan maragul la barku.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg}}

God told Noah to make the boat about 140 meters long, 23 meters wide, and 13.5 meters high. It was to be made of wood and have three levels, many rooms, a roof, and a window. The boat would keep Noah, his family, and two of every kind of animal safe during the flood.

Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Noe, a wana, “Hiyay kayadetan nin bangka a diyagen mo, maghay gatoh boy apatapo a mitodo ye kadang na, lowampo boy tatloy mitodo ye bilal na. Ket katagay na, mapo boy tatlo a mitodo boy tatlon latop ye baag na, malabong ye hilid na boy maghan bengat ye ilwangan na. Ket yatin bangka, pawa kayoy kahangkapan na.” Ket yabaytew ye tikih nan Apo Dioh a impatanda na kanan Noe. Kaya-bay makaoli kananyatin mayadet a bangka, makaligtah ye papamilya nan Noe boy kaganaan a kalahin ayop.

Sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe, “Ya sukad barkuy daygên mu, dinalan buy apattapuy metru ya karang, luwampu buy tatluy metru ya lapad, buy labin tatlu buy kapakay metru ya tag-ay na. Dyag sa kayuy barkuy daygên mu. Daygên mun tatluy palapag ga ati, malakêy kuwartu, buy gisay bubung.” Abiin na planuy indin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe. Kabay sa kapamilatan maragul la barkun ati, miligtas sa pamilyan Noe buy ya kaganawan kalasin ayup.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg}}

Noah obeyed God. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them. It took many years to build the boat, because it was so big. Noah warned the people about the coming flood and told them to turn to God, but they just laughed at him.

Ket dinyag nan Noe ye impadyag nan Apo Dioh kana. Nanaglap hilay tatlon lalakin aanak na ta omen matopad ye an-ipadyag nan Apo Dioh. Ket oli ha kayadetan nin bangka a andiyagen nan Noe, ket taon po ye napalabah bayo na ya nayadi. Boy legan andyagenlan mitatabapa ye bangka, ket hinabi nan Noe kanlan tatao a main lomateng a mayadet a lanab. Boy hinabi na po a homaley hilan oman kanan Apo. Noba adey hila mamteg kana ye tatao!


Dinyag yabay Noe ya in-utus Bapan Namalyari kana. Sinawpan yan tatluy anak nay lawyaki amên masunul la pan-ipadyag Bapan Namalyari kana. Kaban pandaygên lay barku, sinabin Noe sa mani tawu, “Dilag lumatêng nga bapan dagul lanab. Sêpat kaw manyawad tawad kan Bapan Namalyari.” Nuwa asê naniwala kanay mani tawu. Gawan kakaragulan barku, dakun umnuy tawun na nilabas bayu nayari ya barku.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg}}

Noah and his family also gathered enough food for themselves and the animals. When everything was ready, God told Noah it was time for him, his wife, his three sons, and their wives to get into the boat—eight people in all.

Ha nayadi anay bangka, hinabi nin Apo Dioh kanan Nowi, a wana, “Homakay kawoyna ha bangka,” wana. 

____________

Hiyay Apo Dioh, intobol nayay Noen manipon nin malabong a pamamangan a kanen la boy kanen nin aayop la. Ket ha naitaladan anan kaganaan, hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Noe, a wana, "Lomoob kawoyna ha bangka." Ha pamakange na nin hinabi nin Apo Dioh, ket nilomoob hilaynan walon mipapamilya.

In-utus êt Bapan Namalyari kallan Noe ya, “Manipun kaw malakêy kanên para kamuyu buy para sa mani ayup.” Sên nakal-an nay kaganawan, sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe, “Noe ya, sumun kay na sa barku, kaawyun na asawa mu buy ya tatluy anak mu buy mani asawa la.” Para yabay bayduy dinyag la. Wawalu silay sinumun sa barku.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg}}

God sent a male and a female of every animal and bird to Noah so they could go into the boat and be kept safe during the flood. God sent seven males and seven females of every type of animal which could be used for sacrifices. When they were all in the boat, God Himself closed the door.

“Ha kaganaan a kalahin aayop, mangwa kan maghay padih ta ihakay mo hila ha bangka, noba pitoy padih ye kowen mo ha kaganaan a kalahin aayop a malyadin mon maydigalo kangko. Mangikalga kawon malabong a pamamangan yo boy pamamangan lan hilain a aayop.” 

_______________

Boy pinaloob na met nin Apo Dioh ha bangka nan Noe ye kaganaan a kalahin lalakin aayop boy kaganaan a kalahin babayin aayop. Boy namakaget ya po ye Apo Dioh kanan Noe nin piton lalakin mamanokmanok boy piton babayi a iloob na ha bangka. Ta ihagpa nan idigalo. Ket ha makaloob anan kaganaan, inlanab nan Apo Dioh ye ilwangan nin bangka.

Impalakun Bapan Namalyari sa barkun Noe ya kaganawan kalasin ayup buy uybun manuk, liyaki buy babayi. Impalaku êt Bapan Namalyari ya ti-pituy lawyaki buy ti-pituy babayi sa kaganawan kalasin ayup pa malyarin iandug kana. Sên nakasun nay kaganawan, in-saray nan Bapan Namalyari ya pasbul barku.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg}}

Then it began to rain, and rain, and rain. It rained for forty days and forty nights without stopping! Water also came gushing up out of the earth. Everything in the whole world was covered with water, even the highest mountains.

Haanin, ha nakahakay hilayna, hiyay polta nin yabaytew a bangka, inleneb na yan Apo Dioh. Pagkayadi yatew, nangodan yaynan nang-odan, mangaamot boy madeglem angga ha maabot ye apatapo a mangaamot.

____

Ket ha ikaanem a mangaamot, ikana nayna ye odan anggan naabot ye 40 a mangaamot boy 40 a madeglem. Ket hilay dadanoh a andomawoy ihti ha lota, ket nalipohan nay matiboen logal, boy nilipohan nay mangatatagay a mangatotondol.

Pamakayarin anêm ma allu, nanguran sadyay lakas. Asê tinunggên na uran sa lalên apattapuy allu buy apattapuy yabi. Buy alwa pun êt kay abiin, ta dilag êt lanêm ma naubat sa lalên luta. Kabay inlanab ba buun babun luta buy inlumbug ga mani tawugtug.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg}}

Everything that lived on the dry land died, except the people and animals who were in the boat. The boat floated on the water and everything inside the boat was kept safe.

Hiyay babe lota, natagpenan nin lanom. Ket hilay mangapapantay, ahe anan makit. Naoboh anan natey ye kaganaan a tataon ahe namteg kanan Apo Dioh. Ket hilay angkabi-ay bengat, hilan Nowi boy hilatew a aayop a anti ihtew ha bangka.

________

Ket hilay angkabi-ay ihti ha babe lota a ahe hinomakay ha bangka nan Noe, ket nangamatey hilan kaganaan. Hilay napatla bengat a angkabi-ay, hilay anti ha loob nin bangka nan Noe. Ket hilan kaganaan a nilomoob ha bangka na, ket naligtah hila.

Nangamamati ya kaganawan tawu buy ayup. Ya nitagan tanay nabyay, abitu silay idi sa lalên barkun Noe. Lêpaw-lêpaw wa barkun Noe sa lanab. Ya kaganawan nakasakay baydu, niligtas sa bapan dagul lanab.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg}}

After the rains stopped, the boat floated on the water for five months, and then one day the boat rested on the top of a mountain. But the world was still covered with water. After three more months, the tops of the mountains were visible.

Ha loob nin anem a boan, hiyay babe lota, natagpenan nin lanom. Haanin, hiyay Apo Dioh, namaangin yan makhaw ta omon ya omababe ye lanom. Ha naabot yay ikapiton boan, hiyay bangka a anhakyan nan Nowi, nitanghad ya ha mabakil a pinanangalanan Mapantay nan Ararat.

______

Ha pangombah nin odan, naabot po nin limay bowan ye pangomyak nin lanom nin lanab. Noba ha maghan mangaamot, nipatanghad yay bangka nan Noe ha maghan matagay a matondol. Noba ha kanayon a papahen nin logal, ket main po mangalale. Boy tatloy bowan met ye nalabah ha nakit nan Noe a nilomtaw ye mangatotondol.

Sên tinunggên nay uran, limay buwan pun bayu inyumupay lanab. Gisay allu, nisadsad da barkun Noe sa gisay matag-ay ya bung-uy. Nuwa matag-ay pun na lanêm sa kaatag ga lugal sa babun luta. Tatluy buwan pun na nilabas bayu nakitan lan Noe ya tawugtug mani bung-uy.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg}}

After forty more days, Noah sent out a bird called a raven to see if the water had dried up. The raven flew back and forth looking for dry land, but it could not find any.

Ha nalabah ye ikamapo a boan, nakit laynay mabakil. Ket hiyay dinyag nan Nowi, pinalpad na ya ye maghay owak, noba ahe yaynan nag-odong.

_______

Pangalabah nin apatapo a mangaamot, hiyay dinapat nan Noe, pinalpad na yay maghan owak ta omen na matandaan no main anan namal-an a babe lota. Noba panlolompad naynan bengat ta homain yapo makitan namal-an a malyadi nan hagpaan.

Pamakalibas sa êt apattapuy allu, nipalpad gisay uwak si Noe amên matandan na nu namalay nay luta. Kapanlimbadlimbad uwak, nuwa ayin yan nakitan na namalay luta.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg}}

Later Noah sent out a bird called a dove. But it also could not find any dry land, so it came back to Noah. A week later he sent the dove out again, and it came back with an olive branch in its beak! The water was going down, and the plants were growing again!

Haanin, intobol na ya ye maghay kalapati. Hiyay kalapati, tampol yan nag-odong ta homain ya po mahagpaan boy homain ya po maaponan ta pawa po lanom ye babe lota.

_____

Ket ahe naboyot, pinalpad nayan Noe ye maghan kalapati a makew mamilew no namal-an anay lota. Noba nag-odong ya met ateed ta homain ya po mahagpaan a mamalan lota. Ket ha pangaabot nin piton mangaamot, pinalpad nayan oman ye kalapati a makew anaman mamilew no namal-an anay babe lota. Papainghan makandi, anti yayna a ampangaget nin hadiwa a hangan nalaphing. Ket naaliket yay Noe, oli ha hangan hadiwa a kaget nin kalapati. Ta yabay-in yay pagkakitan a namal-an anay babe lota oli ha lanab boy pagkakitan na antomobo anan oman ye ilamon.

Asê naêpêng, namipalpad da êt gisay patipati si Noe. Nangamut nag-udung nga ati kan Noe gawan ayin yan nakitan na sangan kayuy mapag-ugpan na. Sên nilabas sa pituy allu, namipalpad da êt si Noe un patipati. Asê naêpêng, nag-udung yan nigêgtan bulung kayu. Natula si Noe gawan natandan nay manyumêpay nay lanêm buy mantumubuy nan manguman na mani tanaman.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg}}

Noah waited another week and sent out the dove a third time. This time, it did not come back. The water had dried up!

Pagkalabah anan pitoy mangaamot, intobol na yan oman ye kalapati. Tampol yan nag-odong a ampangaget boong kayo. Pangayadi anan pitoy mangaamot, intobol na yayna man yatew a kalapati, noba hiyay kalapati, ahe yaynan nag-odong.

_____


Hiyay Noe, nangagad yaynaman nin maghay dominggo, ket pinalpad nayay naman oman ye kalapati. Noba agyay nag-odong. Ket oli ha ahe na pag-odong nin kalapati, ket inihip nan Noe a main yaynan mahagpaan. Ha wanabay, natandaan nan Noe a madanon anan malabah ye lanab.

Namalibas sa êt gisay duminggu si Noe bayu wi na êt namipalpad patipati. Asina nag-udung nga patipati. Kabay naisip Noe, “Ah, dilag gina lawês napag-ugpan! Ya labay sabin, marani yan mamalay luta!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg}}

Two months later God said to Noah, “You and your family and all the animals may leave the boat now. Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth.” So they obeyed and left the boat.

Haanin, nakit nan Nowi a hiyay babe lota, namal-an ana. Hinabi nin Apo Dioh kanan Nowi, a wana, “Omalih kawoyna ha loob bangka.”

_______

Ha pangalabah nin loway bowan, hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan Noe, a wana, "Noe, malyadi kawoynan lomwah ha bangka a mipapamilya boy kaganaan a aayop mo. Ta ponoen yon tatao boy aayop ye matiboen logal." Ha pamakange nan Noe ye habi nin Apo Dioh, ket nilomwah hilaynan kaganaan a anti ha bangka.

Pamakalibas luway buwan, sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe, “Noe ya, malyari kaw nan umawas sa barku. Paawasên mu êt ta pamilya mu buy kaganawan ayup. Pakapnun yun tawu buy ayup pa buun babun luta.” Inumawas sabay sa barku silan Noe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg}}

After he got off the boat, Noah built an altar and sacrificed some of each kind of animal which could be used for a sacrifice. God was happy with the sacrifice and blessed Noah and his family.

Ket hilan Nowi, lomombah hilayna ha bangka palakew ha lota. Hiyay ona lan dinyag, nagpahalamt hila kanan Apo Dioh ha pangiligtah na kanla. Namatey hilan maghay topa ket in-olam la ya bilang pamahalamat la kanan Apo Dioh ta in-ligtah na hila.

_______

Ha anti hilaynan kaganaan ha ilwah, nanyag yay Noe nin pangihagpaan nin idigalo kanan Apo Dioh. Ket nangidigalo ya kanan Apo Dioh nin kaganaan a kalahin a ayop. Ket naaliket yay Apo Dioh kanan Noe. Kaya-bay in-inged na ya boy hilay papamilya na.

Pamakaawas lan Noe sa barku, nanyag yan gisay pamiandugan. Namiandug yan dakun umnuy ayup sa kaganawan kalasin ayup pa malyarin iandug kan Bapan Namalyari. Natula si Bapan Namalyari sa mani in-andug Noe. Kabay pinagpala na si Noe buy pamilya na.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg}}

God said, “I will never again curse the ground because of humans or destroy the world with a flood, even though humans are sinful from their youth.”

Haanin, hinabi nin Apo Dioh kanla, a wana, “Hiyay babe lota, ahe koynan omanen lanaben.

______

Boy nangpangako yay Apo Dioh kanan Noe, a wana, "Agkoynan omanen lanaben ye babe lota oli ha dadapat nin tatao. Boy agkoyna met omanen damaen nin lanab ye matiboen logal agya po man mag-ilyadin magkahalanan ye tatao."

Sinabin Bapan Namalyari kan Noe, “Noe ya, pan-ipangaku ku kamuy a kina umanên lanabên na babun luta. A kina sirên na babun luta sa kapamilatan lanab agyan makasalanan pun na mani tawu.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg}}

God then made the first rainbow as a sign of His promise. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what He promised and so would His people.

Manyag akon bowakaw a pagkakitan kanlan tatao a hiyay babe lota, ahe koynan omanen lanaben.”

_______________

Ha pamigantan nin kaganaan a nalyadi, nanyag yan maghan bowakaw a pagkakitan nin pangako na kanan Noe a aghe ana maoman yatin nalyadi. Kaya-bay no main hilan makit a bowakaw, angkaihipan nan Apo Dioh boy hilay tatao ye pangako na.

Pamakayari, namipakit si Bapan Namalyari un buwangaw kan Noe bilang tanda' sa pangaku na. Kabay balang magpakit ta buwangaw, maganakan Bapan Namalyari buy mani tawu ya pangaku na.

//A Bible story from: Genesis 6-8//

**Tema: Kalok-an nin tatao**

**Tetepet:**

1) Hinyay maadal tawo tongkol kanan Satanas ha dinyag nin bikat?

2) Hinya hilay pang-oyoy nin Satanas kantawo haanin?

3) Way-omen tawo labanan ye pang-oyoy nin Satanas kantawo?

**Tongkol kanan Apo Jesus:**
Hika boy hiyay babae ket mapayaway kawo. Hiyay lahi mo, boy lahi na,
lanang hilan mapaylaban. Hiyay lahi na (Jesus) ye mamitpit ha ō mo,
ket hika ye manoka ha bitih na. (Genesis 3:15)

====== 2. Nagkahalanan ye tao ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?direct&}}

Mahayaghag hilan mahayaghag ye miahawan Adan. Angkomonin hila ha matampa a pananamanan a dinyag nan Apo Dioh a impabaala na kanla. Loh-ok hila po hatew, noba ahe hila angkading-ey, ta homain po kahalanan ha babe-lota. Maheheg hilan ampakew ha pananamanan boy ampakitongtong kanan Apo Dioh. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg?direct&}}

Noba hiyay Satanas a mapanalingo, nag-ilyadi yan bikat ha panamanan. Ha maghay mangaamot, nanepet ya kanan babayi, a wana, “Peteg laweh a hinabi nin Dioh kanyo a ‘Adi yo angkanen ye agya hinyaman a dawan kayo ihti’?”(Genesis 3:1).

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg?direct&}}

Nakibat yay babayi, a wana, “Ahe! Malyadi min kanen ye hinyaman a dawan kayo, powedan bengat ha dawan kayon ampakaibyay tinanda ha manged boy maloke. Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kammi a, ‘Adi yo angkanen yatew o kimpaan man bengat, ta matey kawo.’”(Genesis 3:2-3))

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg?direct&}}

Hinabi nan bikat a wana, “Hi! Aliwan peteg yatew! Ahe kawo matey. Ahe  na labay nin Dioh a kanen yo yain, ta no kanen yo, mag-ilyadi kawo omen kana a tanda nay  manged boy maloke. (Genesis 3:4-5))

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?direct&}}


Haanin, ha binilew nan Eva yatew a dawan kayo, labay na yan tawayan ta angkainaman nan kanen. Boy labay nay mag-ilyadin madonong. Kaya-bay, namoti ya boy kinan na. Binyan na ya met ye ahawa na ket nangan ya met.(Genesis 3:6))

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg?direct&}}

Ha nakan layna, natandaan la ye manged boy maloke boy naedepan la a loh-ok hila. Ket hiyay dinapat la, nangwa hilan bobōng nin igoh a pinamahanip lan laman la.(Genesis 3:7)


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg?direct&}}

Haanin, nange la a angkomodang yay Apo Dioh ha pananamanan. Kaya-bay nagtayo hila. Noba iningat na yan Apo Dioh ye lāki a wana, “Way-ihtew ka?” 

Nakibat yay Adan, a wana, “Apo, nange katan angkomodang ha pananamanan. Nalimo ko kamo ta loh-ok ako. Kaya-bay nagtayo ko.”(Genesis 3:8-10))

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg?direct&}}

Tinepet nayay naman Apo Dioh, a wana, “Hinyay naghabi kamo a loh-ok ka? Nangan ka nayi nin dawan kayon imbawal ko kammo a kanen?” 

Nakibat yay Adan a wana, “Awo, nangan ako. Ta pinakan nakon babayin in-ibyay mon paglamo ko.” 

Tinepet naya met Apo Dioh ye babayi a, “Taket ta dinyag moy wanabay?” 

Nakibat yay Eva a, “Hi! Hiyay bikat kayabay ay, ta tinalingo na ko.”(Genesis 3:11-13))


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg?direct&}}

Hinabi nan Apo Dioh kanan bikat, “Oli ha dinyag mo, padohaan kata. Ha kaganaan a aayop, hikan bengat ye magdiha nin yatin hompa. Legan angkabi-ay ka, domakap ka boy towapok ye kanen mo. Hika boy hiyay babayi, ket paykahologen ko. Wanabay met kanlan aanak mo boy aanak na. Magha kanlan lahi nan babayi ye mamitpit nin ō mo. Ket hika, tokaen moy tiked na.”(Genesis 3:14,15b))


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?direct&}}

Hinabi na met Apo Dioh kanan babayi, “Ha pagboktot mo, magdiha kan idap. Ha panganak mo, mananam kan tobat a ilab. Agya wanabay man, labay mo po ateed ye ahawa mo, ket hiyay ahawa mo ye manakop kamo.”(Genesis 3:15a, 16))

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?direct&}}

Hinabi na met Apo Dioh kanan lāki a, “Oli ta hiyay ahawa mo ye hinombong mo, dinan ko boy kinan yoy dawan imbawal ko kanyo, ihompa koy lota. Kaya-bay legan angkabi-ay ka, mag-idap kan maggabyon ha lota, a pamopolan mon kanen mo. Ket oli ta ibat ka ha lota, no matey ka, mag-odong ka met ateed ha lota.” Ket hiyay Adan, pinangalanan na yay ahawa na nin “Eba”. Hiyay labay nan habiyen, “Mamyay nin bi-ay”, ta hiyay Eba ye indo nin kaganaan a tatao. Pangayadi, binyan na hilan Apo Dioh nin bado a niyadi ha katat ayop.(Genesis 3:17-21))


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?direct&}}


Hinabi nan Apo Dioh, “Hiyay tao, omen hilayna kantawo, ta tanda layna ye manged boy maloke. Katapolan a ahe ya mapalobohan a mangan nin dawan kayon ampakaibyay nin bi-ay, ta ahe yayna matey.” Kaya-bay hiyay Apo Dioh, pinaalih na hilayna ha pananamanan nin Eden. Pangayadi, hiyay Apo Dioh, nangikonin ya ihtew nin mak-haw a pānobolen, a magbantay, ta omen homain makahaley ha kayon ampakaibyay nin bi-ay.((Genesis 3:22-24))

2.12 Hinabi nan Apo Dioh, “Hiyay tao, omen hilayna kantawo, ta tanda layna ye manged boy maloke. Katapolan a ahe ya mapalobohan a mangan nin dawan kayon ampakaibyay nin bi-ay, ta no makan la, ahe hilayna matey.” Kaya-bay pinaalih na hilaynan Apo Dioh ha pananamanan a Eden. Pangayadi, hiyay Apo Dioh, nangikonin ya ihtew nin pānobolen a magbantay ta-omen homain makahaley ha kayon ampakaibyay nin bi-ay.(Genesis 3:22-24))
